Split Drop Zone Style

When dragging a tab onto a pane, how should split direction be indicated?

Pane Content
↑ TOP
↓ BOTTOM
← LEFT
→ RIGHT

A. Edge Zones

Pane is divided into 5 zones: top, bottom, left, right, center. Drag to an edge to split in that direction. Center = no split (just reorder). Used by VS Code.

← Split Left
Split Right →

B. Half Zones (Simpler)

Pane splits into just left/right or top/bottom halves based on cursor position. Fewer targets = easier to hit. Hold Shift to toggle horizontal↔vertical. Used by iTerm2.

C. Diamond Overlay

A small diamond indicator appears in the center of the target pane. Drag toward a triangle to pick direction. Shows a preview highlight of where the new pane will appear. Used by JetBrains IDEs.
