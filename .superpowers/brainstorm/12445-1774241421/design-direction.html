C. Warm Minimal

Stripped back, content-focused. Warm accent (amber/gold), very thin separators, maximum breathing room. Feels like a quiet, focused workspace — less UI, more work.

Design principles (shared across all directions)

  • Dark-first, with a fully-supported light mode
  • Empty panes get a close button (bug fix #1)
  • Pane-not-found states replaced with graceful fallback (bug fix #5)
  • Editor supports opening folders/directories (bug fix #2)
  • Pane focus tracking for correct split/close targeting
  • Real keyboard shortcuts that match what tooltips show
