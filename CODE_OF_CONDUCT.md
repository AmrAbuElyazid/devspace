# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Devspace is committed to a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free
community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- being respectful in disagreements
- assuming good intent while giving direct technical feedback
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people
- helping keep discussions useful and professional

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- abusive or insulting language
- doxxing or publishing private information without permission
- disruptive trolling or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions
that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email
`aboelyazid.amr@gmail.com` privately instead of escalating in public issue
threads when possible.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces and project-related interactions.
